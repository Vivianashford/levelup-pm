Chapter IV
The Master Algorithm of Growth

Truth matters. What is true you must believe most, because with faith you will achieve. Your thoughts create your reality.

The universal theory of everything and the master algorithm of growth is:

Beliefs
Who am I becoming?
→
Actions
Take massive action
→
Results
Experience new reality
→
Feedback
Listen and iterate

You must intercept at the beliefs stage, take massive action, line up with GOD, experience results, listen to feedback, and iterate again - each time perfecting the process in God's grace.

Where you are in life - your current situation - it is all what you're choosing. You built yourself and you're solely responsible for where you are right now and your future. There is no external force, no son of Adam, that defines you so no one can be blamed. It's your choice to follow or not.

Understand: you are designed to achieve your dreams but you must fight like heck to break towards them. If you want your heart's goals, turn actions into irrefutable beliefs. Be constantly aware. As humans there is conflict between the flesh and spirit - two forces at war like a waveform.

Most people's flesh is so strong it defines the role, pulling strings. But the spirit is exactly who God designed you to be - made in Christ's image, designed for precious decisions of righteousness. Change is always possible. Use mistakes to help refine you.

Important to remember: Intervene at the beliefs stage by changing your thoughts and beliefs. Take massive action in confidence with your new beliefs. Experience your new reality becoming. Experience feedback and adjust beliefs and actions accordingly.

Chapter IX
The Daily Protocol
6:30 AM
Wake up. Remove blankets as a trigger. Send wife a text about something you're honored about her. Write one thing to your kids.
Morning
Drink green smoothie. Feet touch the floor, put on headphones, listen to music. Take a shower.
Prayer
Pray in the spirit for 1 hour. Read a chapter of the Bible. Read a page of a business book.
Affirm
Say affirmations and visualize. In point form - whatever comes to mind, say them out loud. Pre-read reminders of goals.
30 Min
Intense workout inside. Stretches, abs, legs, or run.
Plan
Review Google and Apple calendar, what needs to be done. Map everything over to planner. Fill in the dates and Core 4.
All Day
Take massive violent action all day long. Execute like you want it. Be ready to look fear and procrastination in the eyes. Make it a race, a dance - feel no pain. Do it.
10:00 PM
Lying in bed, complete notes on tasks. What did you do? What do you need to work on? W/L examination. Sleep time, mood, and what you learned - write it down. How you felt (anger, happy, other).
