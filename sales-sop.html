Arena Sales Playbook

Pipeline SOP — What happens at every stage

THE RULE: Every contact moves forward or gets tagged for nurture. No one sits in a stage for more than 7 days without action. If they stall, move them to Long-Term Nurture. Dead pipeline kills deals.
1

📥 VSL Lead

Trigger: Opts in on landing page
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED
✅ Contact created in GHL with tags: vsl-lead
✅ Opportunity created in Arena Pipeline (this stage)
✅ Confirmation email sent with VSL training link
✅ Video watch progress tracked (25/50/75/100%)
✅ Jean notified of new lead via email
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Review lead info (name, revenue, goal)
📋 Check video watch % - did they finish?
📋 If 75%+ watched: reach out personally to book a call
📋 If under 25% after 48hrs: send nudge message
📊 KEY METRICS
📈 Target: 60% watch 50%+ of VSL
📈 Target: 30% of VSL leads book a call
Max time in stage: 7 days
✅ Exit criteria → Call Booked
Lead books a diagnostic call through the booking link. Move to Call Booked.
2

📅 Call Booked

Trigger: Books diagnostic call
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED
✅ Tag vsl-booked applied
✅ Booking confirmation email sent
✅ Reminder emails: Day before + morning of
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Review their Exit Risk Assessment (if completed)
📋 Review their VSL watch data - what topics interested them?
📋 Research their company (Google, LinkedIn, industry)
📋 Prepare 3 specific insights about their business
📋 If no assessment yet: send assessment link before call
📞 Pre-call prep checklist

1. What's their revenue? (from form)
2. What's their exit timeline? (from assessment or form)
3. What's their biggest risk? (from assessment scores)
4. What similar deal have we done? (case study ready)
5. What's the ONE insight that will make them say "this guy gets it"?

Max time in stage: Until call date
✅ Exit criteria → Call Complete or No Show
Call happens: move to Call Complete. They don't show: move to No Show.
3

📋 Assessment Done

Trigger: Completes Exit Risk Assessment
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED
✅ All 7 risk scores written to contact custom fields
✅ Overall Exit Risk Score calculated (0-100)
✅ Risk level assigned (Low/Moderate/High/Critical)
✅ Detailed note added to contact
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Review the 7 risk scores - identify top 3 problem areas
📋 Use scores to personalize the diagnostic call
📋 If score > 60 (HIGH): this is a hot lead - prioritize
📋 If score < 30 (LOW): they might not need us yet - qualify carefully
🎯 How to use scores on the call

"I noticed your owner dependency score came in at 18 out of 20. That tells me if you got sick tomorrow, the business would be in serious trouble. Let's talk about what that means for your exit timeline..."

Note
This stage can overlap with Call Booked. Some leads complete the assessment before booking, some after. Both paths work.
4

📞 Call Complete

Trigger: Diagnostic call completed
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED (when you move them here)
✅ NDA link emailed to client automatically
✅ Jean notified of pipeline change
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Move the contact to this stage in GHL immediately after the call
📋 Add call notes to GHL contact (key takeaways, pain points, timeline)
📋 Score the lead: Hot (ready now) / Warm (3-6 months) / Cold (exploring)
📋 If Hot: follow up same day to confirm they received the NDA
📋 If Cold: move to Long-Term Nurture instead
💬 Post-call follow-up text (send within 1 hour)

"Hey [Name], great talking today. I just sent over the NDA to your email - quick 2-minute sign. Once that's done, I'll send you the company overview form and we'll start building your roadmap. Talk soon."

Max time in stage: 3 days
✅ Exit criteria → NDA Signed
Client signs the NDA electronically. System auto-tags them and moves to next stage.
5

📝 NDA Signed

Trigger: Client signs NDA on portal
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED
✅ Tag nda-signed applied
✅ NDA PDF generated and stored in R2
✅ Confirmation email sent to client (with PDF download)
✅ Company Info form link sent automatically
✅ Jean notified
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Verify NDA was received (check GHL contact notes)
📋 If company info not submitted within 48hrs: personal follow-up
💬 Nudge if company info is late (Day 3)

"Hey [Name], just checking in - did you get the Company Overview form I sent after the NDA? It takes about 10 minutes and it's the last thing I need before I can start building your exit roadmap. Let me know if you have any questions."

Max time in stage: 5 days
✅ Exit criteria → Due Diligence
Client submits company info form. Tag company-info-submitted fires. Move to Due Diligence.
6

📊 Due Diligence

Trigger: Company info submitted + docs uploading
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED
✅ Tag company-info-submitted applied
✅ Upload Portal link emailed to client
✅ Jean notified when documents are uploaded
✅ Files stored in R2 organized by category
👤 YOUR JOB — THIS IS WHERE YOU EARN YOUR FEE
📋 Review all uploaded documents thoroughly
📋 Build the client's Exit Roadmap:
- EBITDA analysis + add-backs
- Valuation range (multiple x adjusted EBITDA)
- Top 3 value destroyers (from risk assessment + docs)
- Top 3 value creators (quick wins before exit)
- Recommended timeline
📋 Identify missing documents - follow up for gaps
📋 Prepare Call 2 presentation/proposal
📁 Document checklist - what you need

Must have: P&L (3 years), Balance Sheet, Tax Returns
Should have: Org Chart, Client Revenue Breakdown, Employee List
Nice to have: SOPs, Contracts, Insurance, Property/Equipment List

Max time in stage: 7 days
✅ Exit criteria → 2nd Call - Proposal
You've reviewed everything, built the roadmap, and are ready to present. Move to Proposal and the system emails the Call 2 booking link.
7

🔥 2nd Call - Proposal

Trigger: Roadmap ready, booking Call 2
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED (when you move them here)
✅ Call 2 booking link emailed to client
✅ Subject: "Your Exit Roadmap is Ready"
👤 YOUR JOB — THE CLOSE
📋 Present the Exit Roadmap on Call 2
📋 Walk through findings: value destroyers, value creators, timeline
📋 Present the engagement: scope, timeline, fee structure
📋 Handle objections (see scripts below)
📋 Close: send engagement agreement + collect deposit
🎯 Call 2 structure (45 min)

0-5 min: Recap Call 1, confirm nothing changed
5-15 min: Present findings - 3 risks, 3 opportunities
15-25 min: Walk through the roadmap - phases, timeline, deliverables
25-35 min: Present engagement terms and fee structure
35-45 min: Q&A, handle objections, close or set decision timeline

💬 Common objections

"I need to think about it" → "Totally understand. What specifically do you want to think through? Let's address it now so you have everything you need to make a decision."

"The fee is high" → "What's your business worth right now? [Answer]. And what would a 1.5x multiple increase mean? That's the gap we close. The fee is a fraction of the value we create."

"I'm not ready yet" → "The best time to prepare for an exit is 18-24 months before you want to sell. If you wait until you're ready, you've already left money on the table."

Max time in stage: 7 days after Call 2
✅ Exit criteria → Closed Won or Long-Term Nurture
They sign + pay deposit: Closed Won. They need time: Long-Term Nurture with a follow-up date set.
8

✅ Closed Won

Trigger: Engagement signed + deposit paid
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED (when you move them here)
✅ Welcome + onboarding email sent
✅ Phase 1 overview: Alignment (Weeks 1-4)
✅ Jean notified
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Schedule kickoff session within 48 hours
📋 Send engagement agreement for signature (if not done on call)
📋 Collect deposit payment
📋 Set up client folder and project timeline
📋 Begin Phase 1: Alignment
🎉 This is the goal
Client is paying. Engagement is active. Now deliver exceptional value and earn referrals.
9

⚠️ No Show

Trigger: Missed scheduled call
▶
⚡ AUTOMATED (when you move them here)
✅ Recovery email sent with reschedule link
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Wait 1 hour, then send personal text
📋 If no response in 24hrs: call them
📋 If no response in 72hrs: one more email, then Long-Term Nurture
💬 No-show text (1 hour after missed call)

"Hey [Name], we had a call scheduled today - looks like we missed each other. No worries at all. Want to reschedule? I've got some time [tomorrow/Thursday]. Just let me know what works."

Max recovery window: 7 days
Exit criteria
They reschedule: move back to Call Booked. No response in 7 days: move to Long-Term Nurture.
10

⏳ Long-Term Nurture

Trigger: Not ready now, maintaining relationship
▶
👤 YOUR JOB
📋 Set a follow-up date in GHL (30/60/90 days)
📋 Monthly value email: industry insight, case study, or market update
📋 Check in quarterly with a personal message
📋 Never burn the bridge - they may come back in 6-12 months
💬 Monthly check-in template

"Hey [Name], saw an interesting report on [industry] exit multiples this quarter - thought of you. Average multiples for businesses your size went [up/down] to [X]x EBITDA. Happy to discuss what this means for your timeline whenever you're ready. No pressure."

Re-entry criteria
They reach out, respond to nurture, or a trigger event happens (health, competitor exit, etc). Move back to Call Booked.

📊 PIPELINE HEALTH RULES

1. No stale deals. If nothing moves in 7 days, take action or move to nurture.
2. Speed wins. Contact new leads within 1 hour. Follow up same day after calls.
3. Notes on everything. Every call, every interaction - add a note in GHL.
4. Move the card. When something happens, move the opportunity card. The system handles the rest.
5. The pipeline is the scoreboard. Review it daily. If it's not moving, you're not selling.
